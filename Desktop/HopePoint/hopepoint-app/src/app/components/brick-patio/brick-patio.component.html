Welcome to the Brick Patio

The Brick Patio is the welcoming platform at Hope Point. It is located to the right of the main entrance. Anyone is welcomed to visit and learn more about Hope Point, who we are, what our mission is, and how we can get you connected with your fellow church members. Please swing by before or after either service on Sunday mornings. We look forward to talking to you!
