Hope Point Nazarene Tables

"Therefore confess your sins to each other and pray for each other so that you may be healed. The prayer of a righteous person is powerful and effective." - James 5:16

At Hope Point, we believe that people grow better in circles than they do in rows. So on Wednesday nights we gather around tables, share a meal, and build relationship with God and each other. We believe that sustained life change occurs as we are growing in relationship with Christ, and this happens best when we are prioritizing intentional relationships with others who are seeking this same goal. Join us Wednesday nights 6:30pm.
